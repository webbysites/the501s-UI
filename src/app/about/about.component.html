we are the 501s

Arguably San Antonio’s 24th maybe 25th favorite classic country tribute band, The 501s are back with classics of their own. Featuring the sizzling lead guitar work of Travis Vela, sparking keys and accordion from Jaime “the Ace” Ramirez, freight train drumming of Johnny Lightning, bouncing bass lines of Derek Badillo, and the heartfelt crooning of Lead singer Aaron Lee Magaña, The 501s are ready to take their south Texas sound to a bigger audience.
